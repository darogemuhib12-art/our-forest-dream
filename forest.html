It's Always Raining Here

🌧️

The rain never stops in our forest.
It drums softly on the roof, streaks the glass,
fills the silence between us with something whole.

We sit by the window, watching drops race each other
down the glass. Your head on my shoulder.
Neither of us speaking. Both of us home.

what life looks like there

Our Days in the Forest

🍃
🌧️
Waking up to Rain
No alarm. Just the sound of rain on wood above us. We stay under the blanket until the world outside turns silver-grey and beautiful.
🔥
The Morning Fire
You wrap yourself in a shawl. I build the fire. The smell of wood smoke fills the cabin before either of us says a single word.
☕
Tea by the Window
Two mugs. Your hands warm around yours. We watch the forest breathe in the rain and feel no need to be anywhere else. Ever.
📖
Reading & Silence
You read. I watch you read. The rain fills every quiet moment. Neither of us needs to perform or explain. This silence is love.
🌲
Walks in Wet Soil
We walk under the trees even when it pours. Your hair damp, feet muddy, laughing. The forest smells like everything right in the world.
🌙
Evenings with No Hurry
Dinner cooked together. Candles. The dark outside so deep it's like we are the only two people who ever existed. It is enough. It is everything.

warmth in the cold

The Fire We Keep

🔥

Outside the rain falls without mercy.
Inside, the fire asks for nothing but wood
and gives back everything — warmth, light,
and the colour of your face in its glow.

"Someday we will find that forest.
We will build that home from wood and rain
and everything we feel for each other.
And we will never need the world again."
